About Vertex SkyPrint

Based in the heart of the West Midlands, Vertex SkyPrint is a newly established, two-person team turning a shared passion for aviation and drone technology into a purposeful business. Born from years of personal interest and hands-on experience with drones and aircraft, we’ve channelled that enthusiasm into delivering high-quality aerial surveys, photography, and videography services for the agriculture and construction sectors. While we may be a small operation, our commitment, technical know-how, and love for what we do drive us to provide precise, reliable, and cost-effective drone solutions — all while continuing to enjoy the hobby that brought us together.

  • A Lifelong Passion Turned Purpose

    From a young age, I’ve had a deep fascination with aviation — particularly military aircraft — which led me to earn my Private Pilot Licence in my early twenties. Alongside that, I spent years flying model aircraft and drones in iconic locations like the Mach Loop and Cheddar Gorge. After the passing of my father, that passion understandably took a back seat for a number of years. But recently, that spark has reignited, and I’ve made the decision to channel my lifelong interest, skills, and experience into something greater — building Vertex SkyPrint. This business is not only a professional venture but a personal mission to combine what I love with meaningful work that supports others in agriculture and construction.
